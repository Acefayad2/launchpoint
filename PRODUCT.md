# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local SMB owners — barbershop owners, restaurant operators, salon managers, gym owners, contractors, medical practices. Age 30-55, not tech-savvy, overwhelmed by digital marketing. They know they need an online presence but don't have time or knowledge to build one. They're skeptical of agencies because they've been burned before. They respond to proof: real numbers, real results, clear ROI.

## Product Purpose

LaunchPoint is a done-for-you digital growth agency for local businesses. The site exists to convert cold prospects into booked audit calls. Success = a business owner lands on the page and within 90 seconds thinks "these people get my problem, and they can fix it." Services: custom websites, SEO, Google Ads, social media management, professional photography, Google Review NFC cards, sales funnels, marketing automation.

## Brand Personality

Confident. Premium. Results-driven.

Not flashy or hype — earned authority. The feeling of talking to someone who's already solved your problem ten times. Calm confidence, not desperation. The site should feel like money well spent before they've spent a dime.

## Anti-references

- Generic agency templates with stock photos and "we help businesses grow" copy
- Overly corporate B2B SaaS sites (too cold, too impersonal for local business owners)
- Cheap Wix/Squarespace one-pagers with no depth
- Sites that lead with features instead of outcomes
- Anything that looks like it was made by AI without human judgment

## Design Principles

1. **Show the outcome, not the feature** — Lead with what happens to their business, not what tools you use
2. **Earned authority** — Every claim has a number behind it. No vague promises.
3. **Respect their time** — These are busy owners. Every section earns its place or gets cut.
4. **Practice what we preach** — If we sell premium websites, ours better be the best one they've seen today.
5. **One clear next step** — Every scroll position has a visible path to booking an audit.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. High contrast text on dark backgrounds. Reduced motion alternatives for all animations. Touch-friendly on mobile (min 44px tap targets). The audience skews older and may be on budget Android phones — performance matters.
